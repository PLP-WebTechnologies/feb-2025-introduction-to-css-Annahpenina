Welcome to Save Trees Africa, a non-profit organization dedicated to reforestation and environmental conservation across the African continent. Our mission is to plant trees, educate communities, and promote sustainable practices to combat climate change and preserve biodiversity.

Why Save Tree?

  1. They provide oxygen
  2. They provide food
  3. They prevent deforestation
  4. They provide medicine
  5. They protect wildlife
  6. They reduce climate change impact

Tree Planting Statics



In the past 5 years, we have successfully planted over 2 million trees across various regions in Africa. Our efforts have not only contributed to reforestation but also provided jobs and improved the livelihoods of local communities.
